---
title: Invite team members
---

Follow these steps to invite your team into LocalOps.

#### Steps to Invite Your Team:

1. Sign in to LocalOps.
2. Click on your profile located in the bottom left corner.
3. Choose **Account Settings** from the menu.
4. Enter your team member's email address.
5. Pick a role.
6. Click on **Invite member**.
7. Your team member will receive an invite link via email.
8. Once they verify the link, they'll gain access to the account.
