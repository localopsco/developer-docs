---
title: Create environment
---

To create a new environment:

1. Sign in to LocalOps.
2. Click on **Environments** from the navigation pane on the left side.
3. Click on **Create environment** button to create a new environment.
4. Pick Name, Connection, and Region.
5. Click on **Next: Review instance**.
6. Review the environment details, and if everything is correct, click the **"Looks good. Create environment."** button
   to start creating the environment.

It may take anywhere between 18 - 20 minutes to complete environment creation process. You can see its progress either
in Environments section or Ops section.
